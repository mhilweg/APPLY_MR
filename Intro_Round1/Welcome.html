Welcome to this study about decision-making. Several research institutions have provided funds for this research.

In this study you will be asked to make decisions that will affect your payment. You will be given detailed information on your screen before every decision. It is very important that you answer honestly and read the instructions and questions carefully before answering.

All information in this study referring to other participants is true and relates to real human participants. Your final payment will depend on the choices and performances of you and other participants in this study.

You can leave the study at any time or refuse to participate entirely without jeopardy to future participation in other studies. Your participation is anonymous, and all data will be treated confidentially.

Your ID will only be used for making payments, and will not be used for any further purposes. Only anonymised data from the survey might be shared in open science repositories.

Please complete this study in one sitting without switching tabs or browsers. Otherwise, we may not be able to pay you your bonus.
This study has received ethical approval from the German Association for Experimental Economic Research. If you have any further questions or wish to contact the data manager, please email michael.hilweg@uni-mannheim.de. At the end of this survey, you will be automatically redirected to Prolific.
