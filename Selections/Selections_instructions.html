Selection Instructions

The four participants performed in two rounds. In round 1, they had 2 minutes to find as many matches as possible among sets of mixed difficulty. They were each randomly allocated a mix of either 30% easy and 70% hard sets or 70% easy and 30% hard sets. Hence in this round they had to find matching pairs in sets which are drawn from one of two mixes

    Mix 1: 6 easy and 2 hard matching pairs.
    Mix 2: 2 easy and 6 hard matching pairs.

A visual example of these mixes is displayed below. The exact placement of cards may not represent the true positioning of the cards in the round.

In round 2, the participants had 2 minutes to find as many matching pairs as possible among only hard sets.
