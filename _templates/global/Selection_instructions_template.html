Worker Rounds

The four workers performed in two rounds.

In round 1, each worker was individually randomly allocated to either Mix 1 or Mix 2 with equal probability. They had 2 minutes to find as many matching pairs in sets drawn from their allocated mix.

  • Sets in Mix 1: 6 easy and 2 hard matching pairs.
  • Sets in Mix 2: 2 easy and 6 hard matching pairs.
A visual example of sets in these mixes is displayed below.

In round 2, the workers had two minutes to find as many matching pairs as possible in sets with only hard pairs.

In other words, in round 1, workers faced sets containing either mostly easy or mostly hard cards. In round 2, workers faced sets containing only hard cards.
