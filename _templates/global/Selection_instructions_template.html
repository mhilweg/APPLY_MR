Worker Rounds

The four workers performed in two rounds.

In round 1, each participant was randomly allocated either Mix 1 or Mix 2. They had 2 minutes to find as many matches as possible.

  • Mix 1: 6 easy and 2 hard matching pairs.
  • Mix 2: 2 easy and 6 hard matching pairs.
A visual example of these mixes is displayed below.

In round 2, the participants had two minutes to find as many matching pairs as possible in sets with only hard pairs.
