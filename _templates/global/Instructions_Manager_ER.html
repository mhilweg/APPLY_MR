For this study, you will act in the role of a Manager. Your job is to select one worker from a group of four who competed on a task. You will find out more about the task shortly. Background
A few days ago, four Prolific participants (workers hereafter) participated in a competition. They individually completed an emotion-recognition memory task for 2 minutes. Their performance in this task was influenced by their emotion recognition skills.

You will be given the chance to practice on this task. In the next page you will find the instructions and then you will proceed with a practise round. In this round you can familiarize a bit on the task, but your performance will not be part of your final payment. Your final payment will depend on your selection choice. You will receive more information about it after the practise round.
