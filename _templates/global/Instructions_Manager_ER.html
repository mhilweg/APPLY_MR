For this study, you will act in the role of a Manager.

Your job is to select one worker from a group of four who competed on a task. You will find out more about the task shortly. The worker you select will then perform another round of the task and your payment will depend on that workers' performance in this round.
Background
A few days ago, four Prolific participants (herein referred to as workers) participated in a competition. They individually completed an emotion-recognition memory task for 2 minutes. Their performance in this task was influenced by their emotion recognition skills.

In the next page, we will provide you with the instructions for the task and then you will have a chance to practise the task. While you practise, you can familiarize a bit on the task, but your performance will not be part of your final payment.

Your final payment will depend on your selection choice. You will receive more information about it after the practise round.
